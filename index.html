About the Camper

Our 6x10 "no build" camper is designed for those who love the essentials without the fuss. Equipped with a 200-watt solar panel and a 110AH battery, it powers two USB fans, a 32" TV, and interior lights for a comfortable stay. You'll also find an extension cord for shore power, a 10-port 15Amp power strip, and multiple USB ports for all your charging needs. Perfect for a minimalist adventure, this camper uses a 2" ball trailer hitch and is ready to take you to your next destination.
